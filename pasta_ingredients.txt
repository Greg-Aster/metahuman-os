AUTHENTIC ITALIAN PASTA FROM SCRATCH - GROCERY LIST
===================================================
Updated with precise measurements and authentic ingredients

FRESH PASTA DOUGH (serves 4-6)
------------------------------
□ 400g Italian Tipo 00 flour (Caputo brand preferred)
  - Ultra-fine milled for silky texture
  - Alternative: All-purpose flour (texture will be chewier)
□ 4 large eggs (room temperature) - approximately 200g total
  - Italian ratio: 100g flour per egg
□ 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil (optional)
□ Fine sea salt (for pasta water - 1 tablespoon per liter)

CLASSIC MARINARA SAUCE
----------------------
□ 1 can (28 oz/800g) San Marzano whole tomatoes (D.O.P. certified)
  - Look for consortium seal/number on label
  - Brands: Cento, La Valle, Strianese
  - Alternative: Quality whole plum tomatoes (but flavor differs)
□ 6-8 cloves fresh garlic, minced
□ 1/4 cup diced onion (about 1/4 of a medium onion)
□ 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil (good quality)
□ 4-5 fresh basil leaves (plus extra for garnish)
□ 1 teaspoon fresh oregano (or 1/2 tsp dried)
□ Pinch of red pepper flakes (optional)
□ NO SUGAR NEEDED - San Marzano tomatoes are naturally sweet
□ Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

FINISHING TOUCHES
-----------------
□ 1 block (8 oz) Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese (for grating fresh)
□ 1/4 cup fresh Italian parsley
□ Extra virgin olive oil (for drizzling)
□ Coarse sea salt (for pasta water)

OPTIONAL EQUIPMENT TO CONSIDER
------------------------------
□ Pasta machine or roller (if not using rolling pin)
□ Pasta drying rack
□ Large stock pot (for cooking pasta)
□ Wooden cutting board (for kneading)

SHOPPING TIPS & KEY POINTS:
---------------------------
• Tipo 00 flour creates authentic texture - find at Italian markets or online
• San Marzano D.O.P. tomatoes are crucial - naturally sweet, no sugar needed
• Weigh your ingredients! Especially eggs (aim for ~50g per egg)
• Room temperature eggs incorporate better into flour
• Good olive oil makes a significant difference in both pasta and sauce
• Fresh herbs preferred - dried herbs use half the amount
• Get Parmigiano-Reggiano block and grate fresh - pre-grated loses flavor

WHERE TO FIND SPECIALTY INGREDIENTS:
-----------------------------------
• Italian markets or delis
• Whole Foods, Wegmans (specialty aisle)
• Online: Amazon, Gustiamo, Supermarket Italy
• Restaurant supply stores

AUTHENTIC ITALIAN TECHNIQUE NOTES:
---------------------------------
• Ratio: 100g flour per egg (traditional Italian formula)
• San Marzano tomatoes = no added sugar needed
• Minimal ingredients = maximum flavor
• Fresh pasta cooks in 2-3 minutes (not 8-10 like dried)

This list provides authentic proportions for 4-6 servings.
