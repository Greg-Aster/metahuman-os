# Dinner Party Responses: "What Do You Do?"

## Professional Pivot Responses
1. **The Consultant Angle**: "I'm doing independent consulting work right now, helping businesses with [your area of expertise]. It's been interesting working with different clients and industries."

2. **The Transition Story**: "I'm in a career transition phase, exploring opportunities in [field you're interested in]. I've been using this time to upskill and network."

3. **The Project Focus**: "I'm working on a few personal projects right now while evaluating my next move. One of them is [describe a project/hobby/side hustle]."

## Confident Redirect Responses
4. **The Value Add**: "I'm between roles at the moment, but I've been focusing on [skill development/certification/learning]. What I find really interesting is [pivot to engaging topic]."

5. **The Entrepreneur**: "I'm exploring some business ideas and doing market research. Actually, I'd love your perspective on [related topic to their field]."

6. **The Portfolio Builder**: "I'm building my portfolio in [field] and taking on selective projects. It's been great having the flexibility to choose what I work on."

## Honest & Engaging Responses
7. **The Direct Approach**: "I'm currently looking for my next opportunity in [field]. In the meantime, I've been [productive activity]. How's your work in [their field] going?"

8. **The Life Balance**: "I'm taking some time to focus on personal development and family while I look for the right fit. It's actually been refreshing to [positive aspect]."

9. **The Industry Insight**: "I'm navigating the job market right now - it's quite different than it was a few years ago. Have you noticed any changes in your industry?"

## Quick Deflection Responses
10. **The Minimalist**: "I'm between things right now. Tell me about your work - I heard you're in [their field]?"

11. **The Humorist**: "I'm a professional dinner party attendee at the moment! But seriously, I'm working on [brief mention] while exploring options."

12. **The Networker**: "I'm in transition and actually love meeting people like you. What's the most exciting thing happening in your field right now?"

## Tips for Delivery:
- Speak with confidence and maintain eye contact
- Have a follow-up question ready to redirect the conversation
- Keep it brief - 2-3 sentences max
- Smile and show you're comfortable with where you are
- If they probe further, have one specific detail ready (a skill you're learning, a project you're considering, etc.)

## Remember:
Your worth isn't defined by your employment status. You're using this time productively, whether that's job searching, learning, caring for family, or recovering. Own your narrative with confidence.
